# Creovate Global Ltd — Founder-Grade Website Blueprint

## Strategic Positioning

Creovate Global should be positioned as a Nigeria-founded, UK-registration-in-progress product and innovation company.

Recommended headline:
**Building digital products for real-world business problems.**

Recommended one-liner:
**Creovate Global is a founder-led innovation company developing practical digital products across small business tools, commerce, fintech operations, health data, and AI-enabled workflows.**

Important wording:
- Use “Nigeria-founded” because this is true.
- Use “UK registration in progress” until the UK company registration is fully completed.
- Once registration is complete, update to: “UK-registered, Nigeria-founded product company.”

## Design Direction

Theme:
- Dark navy / black foundation
- Gold accent for premium feel
- Blue accent for technology
- Large typography
- Spacious layout
- Rounded cards
- Product mockups
- Minimal but confident animations

Avoid:
- Too many stock images
- “Welcome to our website”
- Generic corporate language
- Calling every concept a launched product
- Overpromising before registration is complete

## Deployment Options

- Upload index.html to Cloudflare Pages, Netlify, or Vercel.

Best next stage:
- Convert to React/Next.js with separate pages, CMS-style product entries, and real product screenshots.

Recommended domain:
- creovateglobal.com

- /products
- /contact
