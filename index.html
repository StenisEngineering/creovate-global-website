Operating Model

How Creovate turns ideas into products.

Our approach is simple: validate the problem, design the workflow, build the first usable version, then improve through feedback.

1

Problem Discovery

We study user pain points, business context, market gaps, and practical constraints.

2

Product Blueprint

We define features, screens, data model, user flows, commercial logic, and launch scope.

3

Prototype & MVP

We build a clickable interface or working first version that can be shown, tested, and refined.

4

Launch & Improve

We release, observe behaviour, collect feedback, improve the product, and strengthen trust.
